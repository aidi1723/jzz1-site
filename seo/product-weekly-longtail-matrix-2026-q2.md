This file converts the SEO plan into a per-product operating matrix. Each product page keeps one weekly batch of 5 long-tail keywords, then supporting guides and internal links are updated around that batch.

## Operating Rules

- Keep one main product page as the core ranking target for each product.
- Use 5 long-tail keywords per product per weekly cycle.
- Update wording naturally instead of repeating exact-match phrases unnaturally.
- Support the product page with at least 2 internal links from hubs, guides, or trust pages.
- Refresh `lastmod` in `sitemap.xml` whenever the page gets a material content update.
