# JZZ1 SEO Keyword Roadmap 2026 Q2

This roadmap is for ongoing weekly long-tail expansion. It is not a ranking guarantee. The operating goal is to push more product-specific, buyer-intent pages into crawl, index, and early ranking rather than rely on broad generic head terms.

## Operating Rules

- Focus on low-to-medium competition buying intent before broader category terms.
- Publish 5 long-tail pages or keyword-focused updates every week.
- Keep one primary intent per page, then support it with close variants.
- Route every new page from the homepage, the relevant product hub, the guides hub, `sitemap.html`, and `sitemap.xml`.
- Avoid duplicate intent pages. Consolidate weak overlaps with canonical or `noindex,follow`.
- Use only `https://www.jzz1.com/` in canonicals, sitemap entries, and internal strategy.
