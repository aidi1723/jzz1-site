What buyers should prepare before asking for an OEM quote

The fastest quotation path is to send drawings, size schedules, or BOM plus your destination market and packing requirements.

  • Window type and opening style
  • Size schedule or PDF / DWG drawing
  • Quantity by model
  • Destination country or port
  • Glass and hardware requirement
  • Finish / color requirement
  • Branding, label, or packing request
