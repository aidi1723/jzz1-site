Export Packing for Aluminum Windows

Export packing matters as much as production quality. This guide explains common packing methods, label practices, and what buyers should confirm before shipment.

Common packing methods

  • Protective film on profile surfaces
  • Foam protection on corners and critical edges
  • Carton packing for single units or grouped sets
  • Pallet packing for container loading efficiency
  • Project labels for opening and room identification

What buyers should confirm

  • Product quantity and opening schedule
  • Glass protection requirement
  • Pallet or loose loading preference
  • Label content and carton marking
  • Photo confirmation before shipment

Why this matters

  • Reduces transit damage risk
  • Improves unloading and site sorting efficiency
  • Helps project buyers manage multi-opening orders
  • Supports smoother handover after arrival
