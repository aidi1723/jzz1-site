Aluminum Window Export Packing Guide

A practical page for buyers who want fewer shipping problems, clearer receiving workflow, and better protection for aluminum window orders.

Common packing elements

  • Foam protection on exposed surfaces
  • Carton packing for unit protection
  • Pallet support where needed
  • Labels by unit, room, or model

Why packing matters

  • Reduces damage risk during long-distance transport
  • Improves receiving speed
  • Helps installers identify the right units
  • Reduces downstream confusion on site

What buyers should confirm before production

  • Whether pallets are required
  • Whether project or room labels are needed
  • Whether special edge protection is required
  • Whether photos are needed before shipment
