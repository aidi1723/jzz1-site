Thermal Break vs Non-Thermal Aluminum Windows

Both systems are used in export projects, but they suit different climates, budgets, and performance targets. Buyers should compare thermal insulation, glass configuration, and total project requirements before choosing.

Thermal break windows

  • Better insulation performance
  • More suitable for colder climates and stricter standards
  • Common in higher-performance residential and commercial projects
  • Usually higher cost than non-thermal systems

Non-thermal windows

  • Suitable for milder climates or budget-sensitive projects
  • Simpler structure and lower cost
  • Often used where insulation demand is lower
  • Still needs correct glass and hardware selection

How buyers should choose

  • Check destination climate and project standard
  • Confirm whether energy-saving performance is required
  • Match the system with glass build-up and hardware
  • Do not compare by frame price alone
