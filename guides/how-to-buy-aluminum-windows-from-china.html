How to Buy Aluminum Windows from China

A practical guide for buyers who want to compare configurations correctly, prepare quotation details, reduce shipment risks, and move from inquiry to confirmed production more efficiently.

WhatsApp Fast Inquiry Email Drawings

1) What information to prepare before asking for a quote

The more complete your inquiry is, the faster a supplier can confirm the right system and a realistic quotation range.

  • Window type and opening style
  • Size or drawing (PDF / DWG)
  • Quantity
  • Destination country or port
  • Finish color
  • Glass requirement
  • Hardware requirement if specified

2) How to compare quotations correctly

Do not compare quotes by price only. Compare the full configuration, because profile system, glass, hardware, packing, and lead time can change the real value of the offer.

  • Compare thermal break vs non-thermal system
  • Compare glass build-up and performance
  • Compare hardware level and brand
  • Compare packing method
  • Compare delivery scope and exclusions

3) Key specifications buyers should confirm

  • Thermal break or non-thermal
  • Profile thickness
  • Glass type and thickness
  • Hardware type or brand
  • Sealing requirement
  • Packing method

4) MOQ, lead time and production workflow

  • MOQ often starts from 10 sets
  • Lead time usually ranges from 7–25 days
  • Production starts after drawing and spec confirmation
  • Packing and pre-shipment confirmation matter

5) Common risks and how to avoid them

  • Unclear drawings: send complete dimensions and opening details
  • Hidden hardware downgrade: confirm hardware specs in writing
  • Packing damage: define carton / foam / pallet requirements early
  • Wrong configuration for climate: confirm insulation, glass, and sealing according to local conditions
  • Missing labels or mixed packing: request project or unit labels before shipment

6) Why buyers work with a factory-direct supplier

Factory-direct communication often makes it easier to confirm drawings, customize sizes, align packing details, and keep lead time more predictable for export orders.
