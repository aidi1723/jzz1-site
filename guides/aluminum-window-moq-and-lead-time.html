Aluminum Window MOQ and Lead Time Guide

A focused page for buyers comparing MOQ, lead time, and production timing before they place an aluminum window order from China.

Typical baseline

  • MOQ often starts from 10 sets
  • Lead time usually ranges from 7–25 days
  • Mixed models and custom finishes can extend timing

What affects timing

  • Drawing completeness
  • Glass and hardware complexity
  • Color / finish confirmation
  • Quantity and model mix
  • Packing and labeling requirements

How buyers can reduce delay

  • Send complete size schedules or drawings first
  • Confirm finish and glass early
  • Group models logically instead of changing specifications repeatedly
  • Clarify packing needs before production begins
