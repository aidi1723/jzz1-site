Indonesia Mixed Window and Door RFQ Checklist

This page targets Indonesia mixed window and door RFQ intent, mixed package quotation prep, and supplier-side checklist searches for apartment, hotel, and commercial projects. It answers the project inputs that should be clear before windows and doors are priced together.

Direct answer

The strongest mixed RFQs in Indonesia usually separate window scope, balcony-door scope, and common-area openings while keeping project city, exposure condition, glazing direction, and packing logic visible in one place. Without that package split, supplier comparison becomes noisy and the quotation quality drops quickly.
