Indonesia Unitized Curtain Wall RFQ Checklist

This page is for buyers who need a more practical checklist for Indonesia unitized curtain wall and commercial facade RFQs. It supports search intent around unitized curtain wall Indonesia, Indonesia facade drawing checklist, and the project information needed before a drawing-based curtain wall quotation becomes reliable.

Direct answer

Unitized curtain wall RFQs become much more usable when buyers share facade drawings, module split, visible-section goals, glazing direction, and project-city context together. Without those basics, the conversation usually drifts into a weak price comparison that does not reflect the real facade package.
