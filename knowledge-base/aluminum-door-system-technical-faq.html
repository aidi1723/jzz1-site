Aluminum Door System Technical FAQ

This page targets FAQ-style and AI-style search intent around aluminum entrance doors, exterior aluminum door systems, hardware selection, glazing direction, and export-project RFQ preparation.

Direct answer

Aluminum door sourcing becomes more accurate when buyers define door type, opening direction, glass or panel build-up, hardware level, and project packing logic before asking suppliers for a final quotation. Generic “door price” requests do not give enough technical context.

FAQ highlights

  • What should buyers define before asking for an aluminum door quotation? Buyers should define door type, opening direction, glass or panel direction, hardware level, finish, quantity, and destination market before expecting a reliable quotation.
  • Why does hardware level matter so much in aluminum door sourcing? Because lockset level, hinge choice, closer requirements, and usage frequency can materially change the system recommendation, price direction, and packing plan.
  • What should project buyers send for a cleaner RFQ? Project buyers should send door schedule or drawings, quantity split, opening direction, glazing or solid-panel direction, hardware expectation, and destination details.
