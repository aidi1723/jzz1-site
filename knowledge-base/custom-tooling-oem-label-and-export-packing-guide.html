Custom Tooling, OEM Labels, and Export Packing Guide

This page is built for buyers who are no longer asking only about a product name. They are asking how to manage custom tooling for aluminum profiles, OEM labels for window and door orders, export packing for mixed SKUs, and repeat-order control across project or distributor supply chains.

Direct answer

Many RFQs become expensive or slow not because the product is unclear, but because tooling, labels, packing split, and repeat-order coding are left undefined. Buyers comparing custom aluminum window profiles with tooling support, OEM aluminum windows with project labels, export packing for aluminum doors, or custom extrusion tooling and export packing usually need one execution logic instead of separate discussions.

  • OEM aluminum windows with project labels

  • export packing for aluminum doors and gates
  • custom extrusion tooling and export packing guide
  • project labels for curtain wall and profile orders

What buyers should send first

  • Product drawings or section drawings
  • Expected volume and repeat-order potential
  • Finish and visible-side requirements
  • Carton marks, pallet labels, or OEM logo rules
  • Destination country, shipment split, and accessory packing needs
