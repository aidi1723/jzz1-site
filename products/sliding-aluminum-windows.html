Why buyers choose sliding aluminum windows

Sliding systems are widely used for space-saving operation, practical everyday use, and larger visible glass areas. They are common in apartments, villas, balconies, terraces, and many export projects where ease of use and controlled budget both matter.

  • Space-saving opening style
  • Suitable for residential and project use
  • Flexible glass and hardware configuration
  • Available in thermal break or non-thermal systems

Configurable specifications

  • Custom sizes and panel layout
  • Single, double, laminated, or Low-E glass
  • Standard or specified hardware
  • Powder coating, anodizing, wood-grain transfer
  • Project packing and labeling options

Applications

  • Apartments and villas
  • Balcony and terrace openings
  • Commercial and mixed-use projects
  • Budget-sensitive export projects

What buyers should confirm first

  • Opening size or drawing
  • Thermal break or non-thermal requirement
  • Glass build-up and hardware level
  • Finish color and packing method

Commercial baseline

  • MOQ: often from 10 sets
  • Lead time: usually 7–25 days
  • Packing: carton, foam, pallet, labels
  • Support: OEM / ODM and drawing-based confirmation

Quotation and export support
