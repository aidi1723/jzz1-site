Why choose thermal break aluminum windows

Thermal break systems help reduce heat transfer through the profile, improve energy efficiency, and lower condensation risk. They are a practical choice for villas, apartments, commercial projects, and markets that require better insulation performance.

  • Improved insulation for cold and hot climates
  • Better comfort and energy performance
  • Reduced condensation compared with non-thermal profiles
  • Suitable for higher-spec residential and project applications

Configurable specifications

  • Profile system: thermal break frame and sash options
  • Glass: double glazing, Low-E, laminated, triple glazing
  • Hardware: standard or customer-specified brands
  • Opening styles: casement, sliding, tilt & turn by system
  • Finishes: powder coating, anodizing, wood-grain transfer
  • Colors: RAL-based custom color options

Applications

  • Residential villas and apartments
  • Commercial buildings and office projects
  • Cold-region and high-insulation applications
  • Markets that require higher energy performance
