Why buyers choose aluminum entrance doors

Aluminum entrance doors are selected for their weather resistance, structural stability, low maintenance, and flexibility in finish and hardware configuration. They are suitable for villas, apartment entrances, and commercial exterior applications.

  • Weather-resistant for exterior use
  • Customizable size, finish, and hardware
  • Suitable for villas and apartment projects
  • Factory-direct support for export orders

Configurable specifications

  • Door leaf size and frame system
  • Glass, panel, or mixed configurations
  • Lock, handle, and hardware options
  • Powder coating, anodizing, wood-grain transfer
  • Packing and project label options

Applications

  • Villa entrance doors
  • Apartment and residential projects
  • Commercial and office entrance doors
  • OEM and distributor orders
