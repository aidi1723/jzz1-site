Why buyers choose aluminum entrance doors

Aluminum entrance doors are often selected for custom size flexibility, finish options, practical durability, and a cleaner project or residential appearance. They can be configured for villas, apartment entrances, side doors, and project access areas.

  • Suitable for villas and residential projects
  • Customizable glass, panel, and hardware options
  • Good fit for modern architectural appearance
  • Supports OEM and export packing workflow

Configurable specifications

  • Single or double door layouts
  • Custom sizes by drawing
  • Glass insert or solid panel options
  • Finish and RAL color choices
  • Handle, lock, and hardware selection

Applications

  • Villa and house entrances
  • Apartment project entrances
  • Residential building side doors
  • Light commercial door applications

What buyers should confirm first

  • Door size or drawing
  • Single or double door requirement
  • Glass / panel direction
  • Hardware and lock requirement
  • Finish color and packing method

Commercial baseline

  • MOQ: often from 10 sets
  • Lead time: usually 7–25 days
  • Packing: carton, foam, pallet, labels
  • Support: OEM / ODM and drawing-based confirmation
