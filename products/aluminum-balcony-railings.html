Outdoor aluminum railing systems for projects

Aluminum balcony railings are widely used in villas, apartment buildings, hotels, and commercial projects because they are easy to maintain, suitable for outdoor conditions, and flexible in finish and design.

  • Suitable for balcony, terrace, and outdoor stair use
  • Durable surface finishes for different climates
  • Modern appearance with lower maintenance than many alternatives
  • Project-friendly for drawing-based production

Customizable specifications

  • Height: according to local standard or project request
  • Post spacing: based on drawing and application
  • Infill options: project-specific structure options
  • Mounting method: surface mount or project-defined solution
  • Finishes: powder coating, anodizing, custom colors

Why choose aluminum

  • Low maintenance in outdoor use
  • Good corrosion resistance
  • Lighter weight for transport and installation
  • Flexible finish and appearance options

Suitable applications

  • Apartment balconies
  • Villa terraces
  • Hotels and commercial outdoor areas
  • Outdoor stairs and project rail systems
