FAQ

  • Do you provide railing systems for balconies and stairs? Yes. JZZ1 supplies balcony, terrace, stair, and related railing systems for residential and commercial projects.
  • Can you follow local code requirements? Buyers can share required height, load, or project standard information and JZZ1 can align the system discussion accordingly.
  • What technical inputs should buyers send before quoting railings? Buyers should send layout or dimension drawing, overall length, railing height direction, mounting method, finish, quantity, and destination details.
  • Why do mounting method and layout matter in railing sourcing? They affect structure, component split, fixing logic, and packing planning, so they should be defined before final quotation.
  • Do you support project packing and labels? Yes. Project-friendly packing, labels, and export support can be arranged based on installation workflow.
  • What is the typical lead time? Lead time often ranges from 7 to 25 days depending on layout complexity, quantity, and finish.
