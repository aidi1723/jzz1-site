Who this product line is for

  • Distributors and wholesalers building repeat-order product ranges
  • Contractors and project buyers working from drawings or schedules
  • Villa and residential buyers comparing style, insulation, and finish
  • Importers who need OEM support and export packing execution

Key configurable specifications

  • Series: thermal break or non-thermal
  • Opening style: sliding, casement, and system-based options
  • Glass: single, double, laminated, Low-E, triple
  • Hardware: standard or customer-specified brand
  • Finish: powder coating, anodizing, wood-grain transfer
  • Packing: carton, foam, pallet, labels

How buying usually works

  1. Send drawings or sizes with quantity and destination.
  2. Confirm system, glass, hardware, and finish before production.
  3. Define packing and labeling for distributor or project workflow.
  4. Produce, inspect, and ship with photo confirmation where needed.

Typical commercial baseline

  • MOQ: often from 10 sets
  • Lead time: usually 7–25 days
  • Trade terms: EXW / FOB / CIF by confirmation
  • Support: OEM / ODM and private-label requests
