Why casement windows are important in Europe

Casement systems are widely used in residential and renovation projects because they support better sealing, flexible opening methods, and compatibility with higher-performance glazing packages.

  • Suitable for villas, apartments, and renovation projects
  • Better sealing potential than many basic sliding systems
  • Supports project-specific glass and hardware configuration
  • Available for thermal break performance targets

Configurable specifications

  • Inward or outward opening
  • Single, double, laminated, or Low-E glass
  • Thermal break or non-thermal system
  • Custom finishes and RAL colors
  • Standard or specified hardware
