FAQ

  • What types of aluminum doors do you supply? JZZ1 supplies aluminum entrance and exterior door systems with configurable size, glass, hardware, and finish options for export projects.
  • Can doors be produced in custom sizes? Yes. Buyers can provide drawings or size schedules and JZZ1 can confirm custom production details accordingly.
  • What technical inputs should buyers send before quoting aluminum doors? Buyers should send door schedule or drawings, opening direction, glass or panel direction, hardware expectation, finish, quantity, and destination details for a cleaner quotation.
  • Why does hardware level matter in aluminum door sourcing? Hardware level affects security, usage frequency fit, commercial recommendation, and price direction, especially on entrance and export-project doors.
  • Do you support OEM and export packing? Yes. OEM support, private-label requests, export packing, and project labeling can be arranged according to order requirements.
  • What is the usual MOQ and lead time? MOQ often starts from 10 sets and lead time usually ranges from 7 to 25 days depending on configuration and order size.
