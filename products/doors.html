How buying works

Clear steps for procurement: send details, confirm configuration, then ship.

  1. Send: drawing/size (PDF/DWG), quantity, destination, finish.
  2. Confirm: system, glass, hardware, packing method.
  3. Ship: lead time 7–25 days; photo confirmation before shipment.

Applications

  • Residential entrance doors
  • Apartment and townhouse projects
  • Commercial entrances and back-of-house doors
  • Coastal and humid climates (enhanced sealing and treatment)

Key specs (configurable)

  • Door leaf: insulated or non-insulated (by climate and budget)
  • Profile thickness: 1.4–2.0 mm typical (customizable)
  • Security: multi-point lock + anti-pry options
  • Glass/panel: IGU / laminated / solid panel (by design)
  • Hardware: hinges, closers, handles by door weight/load
  • Weatherproof: enhanced sealing for wind/rain; optional EPDM upgrade

Options

  • Configurations: single leaf, double leaf, with sidelights (by drawing)
  • Finishes: powder coating, anodizing, wood-grain transfer
  • Colors: RAL
  • OEM/ODM: logo, packing, and spec customization

Delivery & packing

  • Packing: carton + foam + pallet options; photo confirmation before shipment.
  • Trade terms: EXW / FOB / CIF (confirm with destination port).
  • Documentation: packing list, invoice, and requested certificates (if applicable).
