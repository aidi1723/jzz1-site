FAQ

  • Do you support custom tooling for aluminum profiles? Yes. Buyers can send section drawings and target quantity, and JZZ1 can confirm feasibility, tooling, and quotation direction.
  • What finish options are available? Common finish options include powder coating, anodizing, and wood-grain transfer depending on product use and market requirements.
  • What should buyers send before quoting custom aluminum profiles? Buyers should send section drawing, application, alloy or temper direction if known, finish, length, quantity, and any secondary-processing or packing requirement.
  • Why do alloy, temper, and finish need to be discussed together? Because mechanical direction, extrusion feasibility, and surface treatment are linked, and the wrong assumption can change the commercial recommendation.
  • How do you quote aluminum profiles? Quotation is usually based on section drawing, alloy and temper, finish, length, quantity, and destination or packing requirements.
  • Can you support export packing and labels? Yes. Protective film, bundles, cartons, pallets, and project or OEM labels can be arranged according to requirements.
