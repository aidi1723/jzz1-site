How buying works

Clear steps for procurement: send details, confirm configuration, then ship.

  1. Send: drawing/size (PDF/DWG), quantity, destination, finish.
  2. Confirm: system, glass, hardware, packing method.
  3. Ship: lead time 7–25 days; photo confirmation before shipment.

Applications

  • Window and door systems
  • Curtain wall and architectural profiles (by drawing)
  • Industrial frames and channels
  • OEM components for local fabrication

Key specs (configurable)

  • Alloy: 6063-T5 common; 6061 available for higher strength
  • Temper: T5/T6 (as required)
  • Wall thickness: 1.0–6.0 mm (custom per drawing)
  • Length: 5.8m / 6.0m standard; custom length supported
  • Tolerance: per drawing/application (confirm with your spec)
  • Finish: anodizing / powder coating / wood-grain transfer
  • Secondary ops: drilling, punching, CNC, assembly (by design)

Options

  • Secondary ops: drilling, punching, CNC (by design)
