Where custom extrusion fits

This page is for buyers who already have drawings or know they need a non-standard profile. It works well for architectural systems, repeat OEM parts, and industrial sections where standard catalog profiles are not enough.

  • Custom section development by drawing
  • Architectural and industrial applications
  • OEM components and repeat production support
  • Quotation based on real section and finish requirements

What buyers should confirm first

  • Section drawing and application
  • Alloy and temper if specified
  • Length, tolerance, and quantity
  • Finish and color direction
  • Packing, labels, and delivery destination

Available finish and process options

  • Powder coating
  • Anodizing
  • Wood-grain transfer
  • Secondary processing where needed
  • Project or OEM label support

Tooling, sample and production workflow

  1. Check section feasibility and use case.
  2. Discuss tooling, finish, and sampling direction.
  3. Confirm mass production details, then pack and ship.
