FAQ

  • Can you provide a full sunroom kit? Yes. Buyers can share layout and dimensions and JZZ1 can confirm configuration, glass direction, and packing plan.
  • Do you support thermal sunroom options? Yes. Thermal options can be discussed according to climate, insulation needs, and project expectations.
  • What technical inputs should buyers send before quoting a sunroom? Buyers should send layout, overall size, roof type, glazing or insulation direction, included doors and windows, finish, and destination details.
  • Why do roof type and glazing direction matter so much in sunroom sourcing? Because roof build-up, thermal direction, drainage logic, and integrated opening choices all influence the final structure and commercial recommendation.
  • How do you handle shipping damage risk? Export packing can include foam protection, carton, pallet, labels, and pre-shipment photo confirmation.
  • What is the typical lead time? Lead time often ranges from 7 to 25 days depending on layout, glass, finish, and quantity.
