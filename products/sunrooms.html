Applications

  • Residential patios and backyard extensions
  • Commercial outdoor seating areas
  • Four-season or three-season rooms (by system)
  • Coastal and high-humidity environments (confirm treatment)

Key specs (configurable)

  • Season type: three-season or four-season (by insulation requirement)
  • Structure: aluminum system profiles; wall thickness 1.5–3.0 mm typical
  • Roof: glass roof / polycarbonate / solid insulated roof (by design)
  • Drainage: integrated gutter + downspout plan (confirm slope and outlets)
  • Glazing: IGU / laminated; optional Low-E for hot/cold climates
  • Weatherproof: sealing and wind/water considerations per project location

Options

  • Roof: glass roof or solid roof (by design)
  • Finish: powder coating / anodizing
  • Color: RAL
  • Accessories: screens, shading, ventilation options (if needed)

Delivery & packing

  • Packing: carton + foam + pallet options; photo confirmation before shipment.
  • Trade terms: EXW / FOB / CIF (confirm with destination port).
  • Documentation: packing list, invoice, and requested certificates (if applicable).
