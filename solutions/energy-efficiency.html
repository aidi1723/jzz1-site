Where energy efficiency comes from

Energy performance depends on the full configuration (frame, glass, seals, and hardware). Share your market and climate needs and we will recommend a configuration for confirmation.

  • Thermal break frames: reduce heat transfer through the aluminum frame.
  • Low-E IGU options: improve insulation and solar control.
  • Triple glazing (optional): for cold climates and higher performance targets.
  • Seals & hardware: multi-point locking and upgraded gaskets support air-tightness.
