How buying works

1) Send size, drawing, or BOM

PDF/DWG, quantity, destination, finish, and any performance requirement help us quote faster and more accurately.

2) Confirm configuration

System, glass, hardware, finish, packaging, and labeling are confirmed before production starts.

3) Produce, inspect, and ship

MOQ usually starts from 10 sets, lead time is often 7–25 days, and photo confirmation can be provided before shipment.

Procurement information buyers usually need

Decision factors

  • Thermal break vs non-thermal selection
  • Glass build-up and insulation target
  • Hardware level and brand option
  • Powder coating / anodizing / wood-grain finish
  • MOQ, lead time, packing, and destination port

Trust & proof

  • Project references and gallery
  • Factory capability overview
  • Quality control process
  • Certificates and market requirements
