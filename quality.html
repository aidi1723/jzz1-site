Quality Control

A simple confirmation flow to reduce rework and keep projects on schedule.

Before production

  • Drawing review and configuration confirmation
  • Hardware and glass options confirmation (when applicable)
  • Finish/color confirmation

Before shipment

  • Packing method confirmation (carton + foam + pallet options)
  • Surface protection checks
  • Photo confirmation for key items and packing

Packing & shipment checks

  • Packing method: carton + foam + pallet options confirmed before dispatch.
  • Protection details: corner protection, finish surface checks, and label matching for project orders.
  • Shipment photos: key packing photos can be shared before shipment for confirmation.

Buyer next step

To avoid rework, please share drawings, quantity, destination, finish color, and any required certificates or local code requirements. Email is preferred for project documents; WhatsApp is suitable for quick follow-up.
