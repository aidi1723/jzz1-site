What makes a window suitable for typhoon-prone projects?

A typhoon-prone project should not choose by frame material alone. Buyers need to confirm design pressure or project wind zone, opening size, reinforcement direction, glass build-up, gasket and drainage logic, hardware lock points, coastal corrosion exposure, and whether supporting documents or pre-shipment inspection photos are needed.
